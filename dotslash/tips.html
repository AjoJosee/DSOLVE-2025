Energy Saving Tips

Discover practical ways to reduce your energy consumption and save money

Lighting

  • Replace incandescent bulbs with LED bulbs (saves up to 80% energy)
  • Use natural lighting during daytime
  • Install motion sensors for outdoor lighting
  • Use task lighting instead of illuminating entire rooms

Heating & Cooling

  • Install a programmable thermostat
  • Set thermostat to 68°F in winter and 78°F in summer
  • Regularly maintain HVAC systems
  • Use ceiling fans to supplement AC

Appliances

  • Choose ENERGY STAR certified appliances
  • Unplug electronics when not in use
  • Use power strips for multiple devices
  • Run full loads in dishwasher and washing machine

Public Transportation

  • Use public transit for daily commute
  • Plan routes to minimize transfers
  • Consider monthly passes for regular travel
  • Use transit apps for real-time updates

Active Transportation

  • Walk or cycle for short trips
  • Use bike-sharing programs
  • Plan safe routes for active travel
  • Consider electric bikes for longer distances

Vehicle Efficiency

  • Maintain proper tire pressure
  • Practice eco-driving techniques
  • Consider carpooling or ride-sharing
  • Research electric or hybrid vehicles

Water Conservation

  • Fix leaky faucets promptly
  • Install low-flow showerheads
  • Use water-efficient appliances
  • Collect rainwater for gardening

Waste Management

  • Practice proper recycling
  • Start composting organic waste
  • Reduce single-use plastics
  • Donate or repurpose items

Sustainable Shopping

  • Buy local produce
  • Choose products with minimal packaging
  • Use reusable shopping bags
  • Support eco-friendly businesses
